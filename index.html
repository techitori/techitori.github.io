Academic Tutoring

We provide standardized test prep workshops, programs, and resources for schools and organizations as well as individual test prep sessions for students. Dean + Golden Education also offers academic tutoring for a variety of school subjects as well as coaching for student success.

College Advising

We aim to help students build their college-bound identity beginning as early as 9th grade. All Dean + Golden Education college advisees are provided with the right tools and resources to ensure their success in the college application process.

Financial Planning

We seek to help families develop a sound college affordability plan that increases access to scholarship and grant opportunities while decreasing potential student loan debt.
