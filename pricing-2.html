According to a report conducted by the National Association of College Admissions Counseling, principals consider college admissions counseling to be the top priority for their counselors. However, only 40% of schools have a designated counselor whose sole responsibility is working with students on the college application process. With the ever-evolving college admissions landscape, schools across the U.S. are feeling the need to increase their college access programming. However, few have the resources and knowledgeable staff to do so effectively for their students. A partnership with Dean + Golden Education can help alleviate this for your school or organization through our dedicated programs, workshops, and resources.
